THE LACE TOUCH

A departure from tradition, the LACE by Jenny Wu wedding collection reinvents the classic symbol of timeless love. Our digitally crafted pieces are designed with intention, meaning and expression. Made to order, our rings carry the detail and precision that only 3D printing technology can accomplish. Finished off with expert hand polishing and stone setting by our local artisans in downtown Los Angeles. Rings are packaged in beautiful dark wood ring boxes - the finishing touch to your perfect moment.
